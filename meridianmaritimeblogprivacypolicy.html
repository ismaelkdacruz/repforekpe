Meridian Maritime Blog > Privacy Policy

The Meridian Maritime Blog provides up-to-date news, insight and analysis to a growing audience of global decision makers. Central to this mission is our commitment to upholding the highest standards of data protection for our readers. This Privacy Policy applies to all websites and web applications which link to this policy and are published by Meridian Maritime Blog. If you have any queries about how we use your personal information, please contact our Data Protection Officer at Info@meridianmaritimeblog.com.

Information We Collect

This section details the information we may collect about you. We explain why, and how, we use it in later sections.

We use cookies (and similar technologies) and analytics tools across our website and newsletter and print circulation to collect information about you. This information can be used to improve the performance of the site, make advertising more relevant and enhance your user experience. We provide detailed information about cookies and similar technologies within our Cookie Policy; examples include:

Your location

- we use cookies to display the version of the site that is most relevant to your location. For example, if you log in to meridianmaritimeblog.com from the Ghana or other areas in the world, our cookies capture that information used for our semi-annual audits.

Your usage

- we use cookies to understand how our customers use our website and interact with our communications and journalism. For example, we use technology on our website and newsletter, which records user movements, including page scrolling, clicks and text entered. (It does not record payment details.) This helps us to identify usability issues and improve the assistance we can provide to users and is also used for aggregated and statistical reporting purposes.

Your device

- we use cookies to understand what type of device you are using to show you the best version of the site.

Your engagement with advertisements

- we use cookies to understand what advertisements you have been shown, or clicked on, to bill our advertising partners, and to present you with advertisements that are more relevant to you.

Where this Privacy Policy applies

Personal information is collected by The Maritime Executive LLC on behalf of The Maritime Executive Magazine and The Maritime Executive Newsletter. We do not intentionally collect or use any information from children.

Our website and newsletter links to third party websites which are not subject to this privacy policy. We are not responsible for their content, use of personal information, or security practices.
